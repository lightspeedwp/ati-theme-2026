Future Namibia Fund

ATI Holidays has long supported charitable and community-focused work in Namibia, and the Future Namibia Fund gives us a dedicated way to continue that support. Through it, we direct contributions towards projects that strengthen education, conservation, and community wellbeing.

This work grows naturally from the wider Social Responsibility story already woven into ATI Holidays. Alongside mentoring, internships, and staff volunteer time, the Future Namibia Fund allows us to support organisations doing practical, meaningful work on the ground.

We do not see impact as something separate from travel. At its best, tourism can help create livelihoods, strengthen conservation, and sustain community-led initiatives when the right people and projects are connected.

Focus areas: Education • Conservation • Community

TOSCO ReGen Programme

ATI Holidays is also proud to highlight the wider movement towards regenerative tourism in Namibia. Through TOSCO's ReGen Namibia initiative, tourism businesses are helping shape a climate-action model that moves beyond traditional carbon credits and instead supports community and ecosystem resilience on the ground.

This matters in Namibia, where tourism, conservation, and rural livelihoods are deeply connected. ReGen Namibia reflects a practical, collaborative approach rooted in the belief that travel can contribute to healthier landscapes and stronger communities.

For ATI, this sits comfortably alongside the values already expressed across our business: encouraging guests to travel thoughtfully, supporting lodges with strong sustainability credentials, and helping ensure that tourism delivers meaningful long-term benefit.

Focus areas: Land restoration • Biodiversity • Livelihoods
